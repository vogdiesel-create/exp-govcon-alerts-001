Stop Checking SAM.gov Manually

If you are a small government contractor, you probably start every morning the same way: open SAM.gov, run your searches, scan through pages of results, and hope you did not miss anything important yesterday.

This is a waste of your most valuable resource -- time you could spend writing proposals and building relationships.

The Real Cost of Manual Monitoring

SAM.gov publishes thousands of new opportunities daily. Manually filtering by NAICS code, set-aside type, dollar range, and geography takes 30-60 minutes per day. That is 10-20 hours per month spent on a task that should be automated.

What Good Monitoring Looks Like

  • Alerts within hours of new postings matching your profile
  • Filtered by NAICS code, size standard, and geographic preference
  • Competitor win tracking so you know who is winning in your space
  • Historical data showing which agencies buy what you sell
